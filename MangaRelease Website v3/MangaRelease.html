What is Manga?

Manga is basically the Japanese equivalent of what you might know as comic books that we all used to read in our childhood. They have a very unique style which has developed over many years and are slowly getting more unique by the day. We hope you are able to join our amazing community and appreciate the art as much as we do!

What is Manwha?

Manhwa is the South Korean equivalent to manga! It normally contains colored and more detailed art compared to manga but is not as popular as manga's in terms of sales.

What is Manhua?

 Manhua are Chinese comics produced in China and in the Greater China region. Whilst Chinese comics and narrated illustrations have existed in China. They are also in color and are way less popular compared to manga and manhwa.
